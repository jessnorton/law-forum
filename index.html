Our Mission

When the Guide to Law School Forum was created, the main goal in mind was how to create a safe space for future and current students of Law to network, socialize, and learn from each other. As we brainstormed ways to accomplish this mission, a secondary goal was found: to support, encourage, and advise students of color to pursue a career in the legal field. According to data on law school enrollment, not even a third of law students belong to black and brown communities.
